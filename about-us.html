About Us

Sopan

A ladder of success

Sopan institute is dedicated to civil services exam and State Public service exams. Unlike many other institutes where every students is considered as potential customer sopan institute offers free career guidance to all the students who are searching for information on civil service exams and only those are advised to take admission who really need it. Emphasis is given on preparing students for handling adverse examination hall situation instead of just giving lengthy lectures and bulk study materials. Proper time management is the key to every success, here students are guided how to divide limited time in preparing for different aspects of this exam.
We at Sopan institute give due respect to your invaluable time and available resources.

Our faculty members with a high average of experience gives personal attention to each and every student so that he can be guided in prefect direction making use of his limitations and strong points. For preliminary exams thorough study of the subject with logical analysis of facts is provided so that candidates can attempt maximum questions on the basis of logical association. Emphasis is given on selective studies especially for the written examinations.
Walk into our center to analyze your preparation for the coming civil services exams. We provide free carrier guidance to the students who are planning to make Administrative services as their career. All these invaluable services are provided at unbelievably low fees. Make your appointment today to analyze your preparation, get free guidance and plan perfect strategy best suitable to you.
